"Everything, Everywhere, All at once" !!
Directed by: Daniel Kwan, Daniel Scheinert
★ ★ ★ ★ ★
Everything, Everywhere, All at once is a sci-fi, action, and comedy movie about Evelyn Quan Wang. Evelyn is a middle-aged woman who finds out she is the chosen one to save every multiverse from an all-powerful being. While she struggles with multiple issues that first generation immigrants experience, like: financial issues, language gaps, and strained familial relationships, Evelyn learns to access the skills and lives of her alternate universe selves to complete her mission.
