Everything Everywhere All At Once (2022)
Directed by Daniel Kwan, Daniel Scheinert
★ ★ ★ ★ ★
A piece of media that transcends across multiple genres to tell a story about familial acceptance and the acceptance of change. Everything, Everywhere, All at once uses stunning visuals to convey the many "what if's?" that the viewer and main characters may apply to themselves.
Criteria Comments Rating
Plot A good plot, but there are certain portions of the context of the movie which is left in the dark. 9/10
Pacing With the two hours and 19 minutes of runtime it was able to convey the central message of the film. 10/10
Cinematography The effort put into the multiple shots of the main characters traversing the multiverse was an amazing inclusion that made the viewer feel like they're moving through timelines alongside the main characters. 10/10
