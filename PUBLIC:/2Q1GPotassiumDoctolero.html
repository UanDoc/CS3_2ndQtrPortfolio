Everything Everywhere All At Once (2022)

Directed by Daniel Kwan, Daniel Scheinert
Genre: Action, Sci-Fi, Comedy
Runtime: 2h 19m

★★★★½

A chaotic multiversal fever dream that somehow manages to be deeply emotional, hilariously absurd, and visually stunning — all at once. Michelle Yeoh delivers the performance of a lifetime, and the Daniels blend philosophy, family drama, and googly eyes into a masterpiece that could only exist in this timeline.
